**Phase 10 deliverable · Authored 20 May 2026 · Niche, conservative, relationship-driven**

## 1. Market overview

International arbitration is a **niche, conservative, relationship-driven market** distributed through institutions, arbitrators, and law-firm arbitration practices. The market is small (~$8-10B globally in arbitrator + counsel fees + institutional admin fees) but every participant is high-value and slow-moving. Consumer SaaS playbooks, virality, growth hacking — none apply.

**Distribution gatekeepers:**
- Arbitration institutions (ICC, LCIA, SIAC, HKIAC, DIAC, VIAC, SCC, SCAI, KCAB, JCAA)
- Top 100 arbitrators (by appointment volume)
- Big Law arbitration practices (~30 global)
- Boutique arbitration practices (~100 globally)
- ICCA (International Council for Commercial Arbitration), Young ICCA, GAR-listed firms

**Buyer profile**: arbitration counsel preparing cases; institutional admin teams; arbitrators reviewing case files; tribunal secretaries.
