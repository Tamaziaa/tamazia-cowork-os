## 2. Services Description

2.1 Tamazia provides B2B services in four lines:

(a) **Regulatory Signal Scans**: structured assessment of publicly visible signals on the Client's web properties against named regulatory frameworks (UK GDPR, PECR, FCA, SRA, CQC, MHRA, EU GDPR, UAE PDPL, US FTC and others listed in the framework_versions registry). Each scan is stamped with the framework version and review date.

(b) **Search optimisation**: technical and content work intended to improve organic visibility on Google Search, Bing, ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews, within the rules of the relevant platform and applicable advertising / consumer law in the Client's jurisdiction.

(c) **Compliance-led content advisory**: structured guidance on copy, imagery and claims to reduce regulatory exposure across the Client's published surfaces.

(d) **Implementation support**: targeted developer briefs, redlines and acceptance tests for the Client's engineering team to implement changes recommended in (a) to (c).

2.2 Output described in 2.1(a) and 2.1(c) is signed off by Aman Pareek, LLM (King's College London), International Business Lawyer, in his capacity as Founder of Tamazia. It identifies publicly visible signals against the cited framework versions only. **It is not legal advice and is not a substitute for review by qualified counsel in the Client's jurisdiction**. Recommendations should be confirmed with the Client's legal advisor before action where doing so materially changes commercial or regulatory exposure.

2.3 Tamazia does not provide reserved legal services within the meaning of the Legal Services Act 2007 (England and Wales) and is not a solicitors' firm regulated by the SRA.
